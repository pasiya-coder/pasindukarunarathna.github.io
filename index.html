About Me

Hello! I'm Pasindu Karunarathna, a dedicated mobile photographer passionate about capturing nature's beauty and creating meaningful manipulations with my mobile device. Originally from Badulla and currently living in Homagama for my studies, I began my journey in mobile photography after my A/L exams in 2019, discovering its potential during the COVID-19 lockdown in 2021. I specialize in stunning nature photography and profound photo manipulations without the need for heavy equipment. Currently, I'm pursuing a BSc in Multimedia at NSBM Green University, which enriches my creative skills. My work is driven by a desire to tell stories and evoke emotions, all through the lens of my mobile camera.
